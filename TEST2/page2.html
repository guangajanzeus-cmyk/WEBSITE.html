Black Clover

Julius Novachrono: Serving as the 28th Wizard King, Julius is an incredibly powerful mage who wields rare Time Magic, allowing him to manipulate the flow of time by stealing and storing it from others. Despite his high-ranking position, he often shirks his duties to wander the kingdom in disguise, driven by an almost childlike obsession with discovering new and unique forms of magic. Julius is a visionary leader who dreams of a world without social discrimination, a goal inspired by his admiration for commoners who work hard to overcome their circumstances. His deep love for his people was most notably shown when he sacrificed his life to save the entire Clover Kingdom from a country-wide attack, though he was later revived in a much younger, 13-year-old form.

Asta: The main protagonist of the series, Asta is a cheerful and boisterous orphan from the forsaken village of Hage who was born without a single drop of magic in a world where magic is everything. To compensate, he underwent grueling physical training from a young age, resulting in superhuman strength and speed that allow him to wield massive Anti-Magic swords summoned from a rare five-leaf clover grimoire. As a member of the Black Bulls, Asta's "never give up" attitude and sense of justice inspire everyone around him, eventually leading him to master powerful transformations like Devil Union with his devil partner, Liebe. His relentless drive to prove that even a peasant can reach the top fuels his eternal rivalry with his foster brother, Yuno.

Noelle Silva: A royal of House Silva and the main female protagonist, Noelle initially struggled with her immense Water Magic due to the trauma of being blamed by her siblings for their mother’s death. Her life changed when she joined the Black Bulls, where she found a supportive family and developed romantic feelings for Asta, whose determination pushed her to master her powers. Through constant training, she evolved from a timid noble who couldn't hit a target into a fierce warrior, eventually manifesting the powerful Valkyrie Armor and contracting with the Sea God to protect her kingdom. Despite her "tsundere" personality and royal pride, Noelle is defined by her incredible growth and her willingness to fight for commoners and royals alike.

Yuno Grinberryall: Asta’s childhood friend and main rival, Yuno is a prodigy who received a legendary four-leaf clover grimoire, the same type once held by the first Wizard King. He possesses immense Wind Magic and was chosen by the wind spirit, Sylph, which allows him to utilize devastating techniques like Spirit Dive. Though he often appears cold and aloof, Yuno is deeply protective of those he considers family and holds a silent, unwavering respect for Asta’s strength. Later in the series, it is revealed that he is actually the lost prince of the Spade Kingdom, and he eventually gains a second grimoire that allows him to use his true innate power, Star Magic.
