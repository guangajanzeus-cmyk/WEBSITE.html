Suguro Geto


Cursed Manipulator

Suguru Geto is one of only four sorcerers designated as "Special Grade," a classification reserved for individuals with immense power capable of posing a nationwide threat. His inclusion in this elite tier stems from his terrifying innate technique, Cursed Spirit Manipulation. This ability allows him to absorb, control, and deploy an endless army of rogue cursed spirits. Unlike ordinary sorcerers who must exorcise curses to eliminate them, Geto swallows them in the form of compressed orbs, completely subordinating them to his will and building an overwhelming, versatile arsenal. His tactical threat level is defined by his ability to bypass standard jujutsu combat dynamics through sheer numbers and strategic versatility. Rather than fighting alone, Geto can wage an entire war by himself, overwhelming opponents by unleashing thousands of curses simultaneously or using highly specific spirits to counter unique enemy abilities. His ultimate technique, Maximum Uzumaki, further solidifies his Special Grade status by condensing his entire collection of curses into a single, devastating beam of pure cursed energy, effectively giving him both unmatched army-scale utility and immense single-target destructive power.

Yuki Tsukumo


Star Rage

Yuki Tsukumo is one of only four Special Grade Sorcerers recognized by Jujutsu Headquarters, a title reserved for individuals with enough power to overthrow an entire nation. While Suguru Geto is known as a Cursed Spirit Manipulator due to his ability to swallow and command curses, Yuki is categorized by her unique innate technique called Star Rage (or Bom Ba Ye). This technique allows her to apply conceptual, imaginary mass to herself and her specialized shikigami, Garuda, without affecting her actual physical weight or speed. Because this virtual mass defies normal physical laws up to a certain point, her physical strikes carry an overwhelming, devastating force that can instantly shatter the strongest defenses.Unlike Geto, who acts as a master tactician utilizing an army of external curses, Yuki fights as a powerhouse brawler who relies on self-augmentation and close-quarters combat. Her mastery over mass manipulation is so absolute that she can completely ignore the concepts of target-based cursed techniques, making her immune to abilities that rely on defining her physical form. At its absolute limit, her technique allows her to increase her virtual mass to a literal breaking point, collapsing her surroundings into a localized black hole. While Geto’s title defines his role as a controller of curses, Yuki’s identity as a Special Grade is defined by her ability to bend the laws of physics to achieve unparalleled destructive power.

Yuta Okkotsu


Copy

Yuta Okkotsu is one of the four officially registered Special Grade sorcerers in Jujutsu Kaisen and serves as the main protagonist of the prequel series, Jujutsu Kaisen 0. Initially introduced as a timid, deeply traumatized high school student, Yuta was haunted by the vengeful cursed spirit of his childhood friend, Rika Orimoto. This manifestation granted him an overwhelming, seemingly infinite pool of cursed energy, which initially made him a major threat in the eyes of the higher-ups in jujutsu society. However, under the guidance of Satoru Gojo, Yuta learned to channel this immense power at Jujutsu High, transforming from a fearful boy into a fiercely protective and confident sorcerer determined to break Rika's curse. What makes Yuta an absolute powerhouse—and a certified "anomaly" capable of single-handedly toppling a nation—is his incredibly versatile innate technique, Copy. This allows him to replicate almost any complex cursed technique he encounters, provided certain conditions are met, and execute them with devastating proficiency. Even after successfully unraveling Rika's soul, Yuta retains the ability to manifest a "will" of the cursed spirit, granting him access to an arsenal of stored techniques, external cursed energy storage, and fully manifested cursed tools. Coupled with his massive tactical intelligence and an unyielding resolve to protect his friends, Yuta rightfully earns his status as Gojo's true successor and one of the modern era's most formidable sorcerers.


Satoru Gojo

The strongest sorcerer in the modern era, Satoru Gojo is a special Grade jujusu sorcerer and a teacher at Tokyo Jujutsu High. Bearer of both the Six Eyes and Limitless cursed technique, Gojo stands in a class entirely his own — a man so powerful, the higher-ups of jujutsu society both rely on and fear him.

Satoru Gojo

Satoru Gojo doesn’t just walk into a room; he executes a flawless aura-farming campaign that leaves both curses and jujutsu higher-ups utterly bankrupt. From a casual tilt of his blindfold to the mocking disrespect he throws at literal disaster curses, every single movement is calculated for maximum psychological damage. He behaves like a god who finds existence mildly amusing, generating so much raw, unadulterated hype that his opponents are spiritually defeated before he even activates Unlimited Void.

Cursed Technique Reversal: Red (Repulsion)

By pouring positive energy (generated from Reverse Cursed Technique) into the Limitless, Gojo flips the effect of Blue. Instead of pulling things in, "Red" creates a localized, catastrophic repelling force. It manifests as a small red orb that unleashes an explosive shockwave capable of blasting away massive targets..

Cursed Technique Lapse: Blue (Attraction)

By reinforcing the Limitless with negative cursed energy, Gojo creates a vacuum of magnetic-like attraction in space. "Blue" forces the world to correct the spatial anomaly, violently pulling objects, buildings, and enemies toward a single point. He can use this to crush opponents or teleport at high speeds.

Hollow Purple

By combining the attractive force of Blue and the repulsive force of Red, Gojo snaps his fingers to create an imaginary mass that rushes forward. "Purple" instantly deletes and obliterates everything in its path, carving a massive path of destruction through whatever it touches.

Special Grade Cursed Spirits

Born from the deepest fears and hatred of humanity, Special Grade Cursed Spirits are not mere monsters — they are living disasters. Immune to most conventional exorcism, requiring at minimum a Grade 1 Sorcerer to even survive an encounter, these beings exist at the apex of cursed energy. They do not hunt. They simply exist, and everything around them suffers for it.


Idle Transfiguration

Mahito

Born from the accumulated hatred of humanity toward one another, Mahito is arguably the most philosophically dangerous cursed spirit in existence. His innate technique, Idle Transfiguration, allows him to directly touch and reshape the human soul — and since the body merely reflects the soul, he can mold flesh into grotesque, lethal forms at will. He is not evil in the conventional sense; he simply sees human life as a toy and acts accordingly, making him unpredictable, terrifying, and nearly impossible to reason with.

Disaster Flames

Jogo

A manifestation of humanity's fear of natural disasters, specifically volcanic catastrophe, Jogo possesses an enormous reservoir of cursed energy and the Disaster Flames technique. He can summon volcanic meteors, engulf entire environments in fire, and even open a portal to a domain — Coffin of the Iron Mountain — that fills the surrounding area with magma. Despite his overwhelming power, Jogo's pride and rage make him reckless, a fatal flaw when fighting opponents on the level of Gojo or Sukuna.

Disaster Plants

Hanami

Born from humanity's fear of nature's indifferent cruelty, Hanami is a uniquely composed Special Grade who feels no pain, harbors no malice, yet is resolute in his mission to protect the earth by eliminating the humans who threaten it. His technique, Disaster Plants, lets him grow cursed plant life from the ground, drain cursed energy from sorcerers through roots called "flowers," and withstand damage that would obliterate most opponents. His resilience and calm make him one of the most difficult cursed spirits to put down.

Disaster Tides

Dagon

A cursed spirit born from humanity's fear of the deep ocean, Dagon begins as an incomplete fetus-like creature but fully awakens during the Shibuya Incident into a towering, terrifying Special Grade. His technique, Disaster Tides, grants him control over water and a swarm of deadly shikigami called Death Swarm. His domain expansion, Horizon of the Captivating Skandha, creates a perfect enclosed beach world where his shikigami attacks cannot miss — a near-inescapable death trap that overwhelmed three Grade 1 sorcerers simultaneously.

Brain Transplant + Barrier Techniques

Kenjaku

The most ancient and cunning entity in the series, Kenjaku is a sorcerer over a thousand years old who survives by transplanting his brain into new host bodies — each time inheriting the previous host's innate technique. Currently inhabiting Suguru Geto's body, he wields Cursed Spirit Manipulation alongside his own mastery of Barrier Techniques and Reverse Cursed Energy. He is the architect of every major catastrophe in the series, engineering the Prison Realm sealing of Gojo and the entire Culling Game to reshape the jujutsu world according to a plan centuries in the making.

The King of Curses Approaches.

Before you proceed — understand this. What comes next is not a character profile. It is not a wiki entry. It is a record of something that should not exist. A being so far beyond the concept of "cursed spirit" or "sorcerer" that the very vocabulary of jujutsu society fails to contain him. Every sorcerer you have read about above — every Special Grade, every genius, every prodigy — exists in a world that was shaped by the shadow he cast a thousand years ago. He didn't conquer that era. He was that era. And now, sealed across twenty fingers, fractured and dormant within an unwilling vessel, he stirs. The King does not awaken in a hurry. He has waited a thousand years. He can wait a moment longer.

The King in Motion


Ryomen Sukuna

King of Curses. The Disgraced One. The Undisputed Pinnacle.

Over a thousand years ago, during the golden age of jujutsu, one human stood so far above all others that the entire sorcerer world could do nothing but watch in helpless awe. Ryomen Sukuna — the Disgraced One, the King of Curses — was a sorcerer of such monstrous power that even in death, his twenty severed fingers refused to rot, preserved as near-indestructible cursed objects across centuries. Today, reincarnated finger by finger within the body of Yuji Itadori, Sukuna operates not as a villain with a plan, but as a force of nature with a preference: to do whatever amuses him, consequences be damned.

Dismantle

Sukuna's most freely used slashing attack. Dismantle fires invisible blades of cursed energy that cut through virtually anything — flesh, stone, and space itself. He can fire them in any direction, including behind him, without looking, and uses them casually the way most people breathe. Even a fraction of his full power using Dismantle is enough to bisect a fully realized curse or tear apart a building.

Cleave

A more adaptive slashing technique that calibrates itself to the target's durability and cursed energy level, ensuring the slash always cuts at the exact force needed to kill or destroy. Against a human with no cursed energy, it's a gentle slice. Against a hardened sorcerer, it scales upward to devastating force. Cleave makes Sukuna extraordinarily efficient — he wastes nothing.

Flame Arrow (Fire Technique)

Revealed after consuming Jogo, Sukuna gained access to fire-based techniques. He wields them with more mastery than Jogo himself ever could, amplifying them through his overwhelming cursed energy. When combined with his slashing techniques, Sukuna can unleash fire arrows and flame-imbued slashes that engulf entire city blocks in seconds, as demonstrated devastatingly during the Shibuya Incident.

Malevolent Shrine

Sukuna's Domain Expansion — and the most extraordinary domain ever recorded. Unlike every other domain, Malevolent Shrine does not create an enclosed barrier. Instead, it manifests directly in the real world, an open-air shrine pulsing with divine, cursed energy, constantly firing Dismantle and Cleave in all directions within a massive radius. Because it uses no barrier, it cannot be overwhelmed by a binding vow — and its guaranteed hit applies across a staggering area, making it a domain that rewrites the rules of jujutsu combat itself.

There Are No Winners Here.

Jujutsu Kaisen is not a story about heroes triumphing over evil. It is a story about people — broken, brilliant, desperate people — throwing themselves against forces that should be impossible to face. Sorcerers who laugh in the face of death. Curses who were born from human suffering. A King who finds the entire struggle mildly entertaining. Whatever side you root for, one truth remains absolute: in this world, strength alone does not guarantee survival. And that is what makes it unforgettable.
